What cars are most vulnerable

Recent studies have revealed that compact sedans and hatchbacks are among the most vulnerable to theft and break-ins in urban areas. Vehicles such as the Toyota Corolla and Volkswagen Polo are particularly targeted due to their popularity and relatively simple security features. To mitigate the risk of car theft, it's essential for owners to invest in additional security measures such as steering wheel locks and GPS tracking systems.

Statistics showed which average age

According to recent statistical analysis, the average age of individuals renting cars for leisure purposes falls within the 25 to 40-year-old range. This demographic segment comprises young professionals and families seeking flexible transportation options for weekend getaways and vacations. Additionally, the data indicates a growing trend of older adults, aged 55 and above, opting for long-term car rentals as an alternative to vehicle ownership.
